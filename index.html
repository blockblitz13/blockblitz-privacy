Privacy Policy for Block Blitz

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Block Blitz collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

  • Device information: Basic device data such as model and OS version.
  • Usage data: In-game events used to improve gameplay and app performance.
  • Advertising IDs: Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers to serve ads.
  • Financial data: Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store payment card details.

2. How We Use Data

  • To operate and improve the game.
  • To analyze app performance and fix crashes.
  • To show advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To support in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.

3. Third-Party Services

Block Blitz uses Google Play Services, Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google Play Billing.

4. Data Retention

Game progress such as scores and achievements may be stored locally on your device. Analytics and crash data are retained according to Google/Firebase policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

Block Blitz is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
