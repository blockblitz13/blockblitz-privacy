Privacy Policy for Block Blitz

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Block Blitz ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

  • Device Information: We may collect basic device data, such as device model, operating system version, app version, device identifiers, and diagnostic information through Google Firebase and Google AdMob to improve app performance, stability, analytics, and ad delivery.
  • Usage Data: We track in-game events, such as app opens, game starts, scores, achievements, ad interactions, hints, undo actions, and purchase events to provide and improve game functionality. Game progress such as scores, coins, settings, and achievements may also be stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising IDs: Google AdMob may collect or use your Advertising ID and related device identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and comply with applicable policies. Ads are not shown if you have purchased the "Remove Ads" option where applicable.
  • Approximate Location: Google services such as AdMob may infer approximate location from network information, such as IP address, for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance. The app does not request precise location permission.
  • Financial Data: All in-app purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not collect, view, or store your payment card number, bank account, or other payment method details. We may receive purchase status information, such as whether a purchase was completed, restored, or failed, to provide purchased items and support app functionality.
  • Crash Logs and Diagnostics: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports, diagnostic information, and app stability data so we can detect, troubleshoot, and fix technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the game.
  • To save and manage local game progress, scores, coins, settings, and achievements.
  • To analyze app usage, app performance, and feature engagement.
  • To diagnose crashes, errors, and technical issues.
  • To display advertisements to free users through Google AdMob.
  • To process and restore in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.
  • To support update prompts, configuration changes, and app improvements through Firebase Remote Config.
  • To help prevent fraud, improve security, and comply with applicable laws and platform policies.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted third-party services that help us operate, monetize, analyze, secure, and improve the app.

  • Google Play Services – Core app functionality.
  • Google Play Billing – In-app purchases and purchase restoration.
  • Google AdMob – Advertising, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and compliance.
  • Firebase Analytics – App usage analytics and performance insights.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – Crash reporting and diagnostics.
  • Firebase Remote Config – App configuration and update prompt support.

These services operate under Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Retention

In-game data such as scores, coins, settings, and achievements may be stored locally on your device and is removed when the app is uninstalled or app data is cleared. Analytics, advertising, purchase status, diagnostic, and crash data collected by Google services are retained according to Google's standard retention settings and policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Block Blitz is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data associated with your use of the app. To make a request, contact us at the email address below.
